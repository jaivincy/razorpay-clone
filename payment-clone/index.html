QUESTIONS, ANSWERED

Frequently asked questions.

How quickly can I start accepting payments?+

Most teams can create an account and accept their first payment in just a few minutes. Our APIs and guides make deeper integrations straightforward too.

Which payment methods do you support?+

PayFlow supports cards, bank transfers, wallets, QR payments, and local methods across supported regions.

Are there setup or monthly fees?+

No. Starter and Growth plans have no setup fees. You only pay a clear fee when you process a transaction.

Can I accept international payments?+

Yes. Accept payments from customers around the world and settle in your preferred currency.

How secure is PayFlow?+

We use modern encryption, monitoring, and layered controls to protect your data and every payment.

Do you offer recurring billing?+

Yes. Build subscriptions, flexible billing schedules, and automated invoice collection with PayFlow Billing.

Is there a sandbox for developers?+

Yes, every account includes a full test environment with realistic payment flows and developer tools.

When are funds settled?+

Settlement timing depends on your region and payment method, with instant options available in many markets.

Can I issue cards to my team?+

Yes. PayFlow Cards lets you issue physical or virtual cards with granular spend controls.

How can I get help?+

Our support team is available by email, with priority and dedicated support on higher plans.
